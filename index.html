Blood Donation Guidelines

Eligibility & Restrictions for Safe Donation

Before Donating Blood

  • Have breakfast – donation on an empty stomach is not allowed
  • Sleep at least 6 hours the night before
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before donating
  • Avoid smoking for at least 6 hours

Who Cannot Donate Blood

  • On constant antibiotic medication
  • Took medicine within last 24 hrs
  • Got tattoos/piercing within 1 year
  • Unexplained weight loss >5 kg in 6 months
  • Dental procedures or surgeries within last 1 month
  • High BP or Diabetes patients
  • Rabies/hepatitis B vaccine within last 12 months
  • Asthma patients
  • Women during menstruation

Donation Frequency

  • Males: Every 3 months
  • Females: Every 4 months

Requirements

  • Weight: ≥ 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin (Males): ≥ 13 g/dL
  • Hemoglobin (Females): ≥ 12.5 g/dL

💖 Healthy donor = Safe donation!
